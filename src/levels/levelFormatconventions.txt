dLevels format: 

Level name convention: levelX.txt
        X represents the number of level

int type, int position in x, int position in y

type is represented by int number:
    type    values  represents
    
    enemies
            2       Goomba
            3       KoopaTroopa
            4       Piranha Plant
            5       Lakitu
            6       Spiny
            7       Buzzy Beetle

    power ups with question block
            10      SuperMushroom
            11      FireFlower
            12      Star
            13      GreenMushroom
            14      Coin

    power up without question block
            15      Coin

    platforms   
            21      SolidBlock
            22      BrickBlock
            23      Void
            24      Flag
            25      Mast
            26      MastEnd
            27      PipeBottomLeft
            28      PipeBottomRight
            29      PipeTopLeft
            30      PipeTopRight
    projetiles
            31      Fireball
